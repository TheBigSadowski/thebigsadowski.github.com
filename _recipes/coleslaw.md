## Ingredients
* 3/4 cup mayonnaise
* 2 tablespoons sugar
* 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
* 1 tablespoon horseradish
* 1 teaspoon salt
* 2 teaspoons black pepper
* 2 tablespoons white vinegar
* 1 head cabbage, cored, tough leaves removed and shredded
* 2 small carrots, shredded
